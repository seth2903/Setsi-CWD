ABOUT US


Tess Travels is created to provide you with commonly used travel websites for booking local or international

flights, accommodations, tours and others sources of information in just one website for your easy access.

When we travel abroad once a year, we usually do "do-it-yourself" (DIY) arrangements. We book flights and hotels

on our own, even bus, ferry or train transportation. Aside from online resources, we always visit the annual

Travel Tour Expo at the SMX Convention Center every February. There are discounted deals on airfares, hotels,

package tours and even tourist visa fees of Japan and South Korea which we usually look out for. These

have been very helpful in managing our travel expenses.
